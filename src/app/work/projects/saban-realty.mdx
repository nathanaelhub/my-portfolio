## Overview

A real-estate site for properties on Saba — the 5-square-mile volcanic Caribbean island that's also where I grew up. Built as a side project for friends and family selling property there, but also as a small piece of digital infrastructure for an island that doesn't get a lot of polished web presence.

## Saba, briefly

- **Population:** ~1,900
- **Coordinates:** 17.63°N · 63.23°W
- **Highest point:** Mt. Scenery, 877m — and the highest point in the Kingdom of the Netherlands
- **Notable:** the shortest commercial runway in the world

## What the site does

- Property listings with photos, location, square meterage, and a Saba-aware map
- Listing detail pages with neighborhood context (Windwardside, The Bottom, St. Johns, Hell's Gate)
- Inquiry form routing to the listing agent
- Static-export deploy, no backend required

## Why it's here

It's the most personal thing on this portfolio. The work I do in AI/ML is the work I want a job for — this is the work I do because I love where I'm from.
